What is the major problem??

Humans have been a threat to the animal kingdom espicially animals which have become domesticated.
Homeless animals outnumber homeless people 5 to 1.
There are 600 million unwanted animals in the world, and not enough people willing to take care of them.
70 million of those animals are in the US which has a 318.6 million population (2014 census).
Even though the number of people is overwhelming, only 36-47% of households in the US have pets.
So where do the animals come from? Basically there are two different groups :
those who were abandoned by owners (both puppies and adult dogs) and those born in the street.
